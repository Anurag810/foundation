ERPNext Foundation

We are a registered not-for-profit entity representing a community of ERPNext users, developers and service providers.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide high quality free software to organizations, enterprises and public institutions around the world, and enable to use it by spreading the knowledge and best practices of running organizations in the 21st Century.

Our Approach

To enable this vision we will:
1. Create platforms to bring people together to share their knowledge.
2. Create software and documentation so people can be self-reliant.
3. Work with public institutions and help them use tools like ERPNext.

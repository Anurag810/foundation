ERPNext Foundation

We are a Non Profit Foundation representing a community of ERPNext Users, Developers and Service Providers. Our goal is to provide a platform for the ERPNext Community to come together, join hands and build assets to help build and enhance ERPNext around the world.

ERPNext Foundation aims to be a community driven organization and run in a transparent manner. If you want to be more involved in the ERPNext Community, read the FAQ.

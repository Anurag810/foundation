What is ERPNext?

ERPNext is a modern, fully featured open source Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system that is designed for small and medium sized businesses that includes modules to help you manage Financial Accounting, Sales and CRM, Billing, Manufacturing, Inventory, HR and Payroll, Projects, E-Commerce and much more.

Start using ERPNext

What is the ERPNext Foundation?

ERPNext Foundation is a not-for-profit company registered with the objective of building and enhancing ERPNext as an open source project. The ERPNext Foundation is funded by annual memberships of individuals and companies who use ERPNext or support it.
